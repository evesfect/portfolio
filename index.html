Dynamic Terrain System

Terrain-Tools-Unity

I had issues with terrain manipulation while working on a procedural hive city generator, trying to adjust terrain levels according to the building floors. Basic height alignment meant a great sacrifice in quality, and really high complexity during so. What if I could simply include ground patch meshes with the building objects, without rendering any of them in-game, just to adjust terrain real-time?
This one drastically speeds up the level building process, and also supports in-game terrain manipulations with only 1 render pass delay.
